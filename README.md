CleanChain AI 

AI-Powered Cleanup. Blockchain-Proven Impact.

CleanChain AI is a privacy-first environmental impact verification platform that uses Artificial Intelligence, Blockchain, and Decentralized Storage to measure and verify real-world cleanup activities.

The platform analyzes before and after images of waste cleanup, calculates an environmental impact score using AI, and securely records the results on the blockchain, ensuring transparency, accountability, and trust in environmental initiatives and CSR activities.

Inspiration

Environmental cleanup initiatives are increasing worldwide, but verifying the real impact of these activities remains difficult.

Many organizations report environmental efforts through manual documentation and centralized systems, which can lead to lack of transparency, unverifiable reports, and greenwashing.

CleanChain AI was built to solve this problem by combining AI-powered environmental analysis with blockchain-based verification, ensuring that cleanup efforts are accurately measured and tamper-proof.

What It Does

CleanChain AI provides a platform where:

• NGOs and volunteers upload before and after images of cleanup activities
• AI models detect and analyze waste objects in the images
• The system calculates a cleanup efficiency and impact score
• Results are recorded on blockchain for transparency
• Cleanup reports are stored securely using decentralized document storage

This creates a trustworthy system for verifying environmental impact.

Key Features
AI Waste Detection

Uses computer vision models (YOLO / MobileNet) to detect waste such as plastic, paper, and garbage from uploaded images.

Environmental Impact Score

Automatically calculates an impact score based on detected waste removal.

Blockchain Verification

Cleanup results are stored on Polygon blockchain using smart contracts to ensure tamper-proof records.

Decentralized Reports

Cleanup documentation and reports are securely stored using Fileverse decentralized storage.
